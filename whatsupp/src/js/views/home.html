iPhone

  1. Select your desired chat
  2. Tap on their name at the top of the screen
  3. Scroll down
  4. Select 'Export Chat'

Android

  1. Select your desired chat
  2. Pick menu from top right corner - 3 dots
  3. Choose 'More'
  4. Email Chat
  5. Without Media
  6. Send the chat to yourself and upload below
